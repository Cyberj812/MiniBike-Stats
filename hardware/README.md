# Hardware

This folder is for:
- Pinout diagrams (SVG or images)
- KiCad / EasyEDA schematics and PCB if you design a custom board
- 3D printed enclosure models (STL, STEP, Fusion 360)
- BOM (bill of materials)
- Photos of your build

## Example Minimal BOM (no specific display chosen yet)

- ESP32-S3 dev board or module with sufficient pins/PSRAM
- Your chosen display (Waveshare/Elecrow/etc.)
- Level shifter or appropriate logic for display if needed
- Buck converter (5V or 3.3V clean)
- Buttons or rotary encoder + knob
- Connectors (Julet, XT30, Anderson, etc.)
- Fuse holder + appropriate fuse
- Wire, heat shrink, zip ties
- Optional: GPS module (UART), microSD breakout, buzzer, status LEDs

## Enclosure Ideas

- Handlebar clamp like the commercial unit
- 3D printed or aluminum box with IP rating consideration (or use conformal coating + good gaskets)
- Sun shade / visor for the screen

Once you select a display and finalize wiring, add your specific pinout diagram and photos here.
